Non-Fungible Token


Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) are unique digital assets verified using blockchain technology.
Unlike cryptocurrencies, which are identical and interchangeable, NFTs represent ownership of a specific item piece of content, such as art, music, videos, or virtual real estate.They are revolutionizing how we buy, sell, and interact with digital content, offering new opportunities for artists, creators, and collectors.
